Sponsors

Our wonderful partners who support our cycling community and make our adventures possible. We're proud to be associated with these fantastic organizations.

GearShift Dynamics

Premier cycling equipment manufacturer specializing in high-performance bikes and customized gear. Their commitment to innovation has revolutionized how we experience trail riding, making them our trusted technical partner since 2023.

Trail & Track Cafe

Our favorite post-ride refueling station in Fleet! They've been keeping our cyclists energized with specially crafted energy-packed meals and the best coffee in Hampshire. Exclusive 15% discount for all FMM members.

MudGuard Insurance

Specialized cycling insurance provider offering comprehensive coverage for our adventures. Their unique understanding of cyclists' needs has made them the perfect partner for protecting our members and their beloved bikes.

EcoTrail Solutions

Environmental consultancy helping us minimize our impact on nature. They've been instrumental in developing our sustainable trail maintenance programs and organizing our annual forest cleanup initiatives.

WheelWorks Tech

Local bicycle maintenance experts providing top-notch service to our community. Their mobile repair unit is always ready to assist during our group rides, ensuring everyone stays safe and rolling smoothly.
