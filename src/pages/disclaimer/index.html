Group Ride Disclaimer

By joining our rides, you acknowledge that cycling involves mud, water, and the elements. That's what makes us Mucky Mayhem - embracing the outdoor adventure in all its forms!

Our ride leaders are passionate volunteers who know their routes well. While not professional guides, they're fellow adventurers who love sharing the joy of cycling.

Keep your bike in top shape with our ABC check: Air in tires, Brakes working, Chain lubricated. And remember - helmets are non-negotiable. We value those cycling brains!

Weather can be unpredictable. Ride leaders may modify or cancel rides for safety. Remember: there's no bad weather, just inappropriate clothing!

From gentle towpath cruising to ambitious hill climbing - be honest about your fitness level. It's about the journey and cafe stops, not racing!

While we bring the fun, Fleet Mucky Mayhem isn't responsible for tumbles, mechanical issues, or surprisingly deep puddles. Participation is at your own risk.

Be a cycling ambassador - follow traffic rules, respect other trail users, and share those friendly waves. Good vibes only on our rides!

Riders under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian. Young ones enjoy the adventure while grown-ups handle the responsibility.

If you have any questions about this disclaimer or other issues,
please do not hesitate to contact us.
